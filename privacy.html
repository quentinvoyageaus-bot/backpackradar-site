Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address, first name, last name, and password (encrypted). We also collect your preferred city and plan selection. If you upload a profile photo, it is stored securely.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our services, send you job alerts based on your preferences, and communicate important updates about your account or our service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

4. Job Data

Job listings are sourced from Seek.com.au and are publicly available. We aggregate and filter these listings using AI to identify positions suitable for Working Holiday Visa holders. Job listings are automatically removed after 30 days.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage to save your preferences and session information. This data remains on your device and is used to provide a seamless experience.

6. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for authentication and data storage. We may use analytics services to understand how our app is used. We do not share your personal information with advertisers.

7. Your Rights

You can view, update, or delete your personal information at any time through the app's profile settings. You can delete your account entirely, which will remove all your data from our servers.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is permanently removed. Job alerts are kept for 30 days before automatic deletion.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure password hashing, and row-level security on our database.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@backpackradar.app.
